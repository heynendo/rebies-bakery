# Rebie's Bakery

A custom site for a home-based custom bakery, giving the business a professional online presence to showcase their work and attract new customers. Features a full photo gallery of custom creations, a browsable menu, and a contact section for placing inquiries — turning a word-of-mouth business into one that's discoverable online.

---
## Features

**Bakery Menu**: Full menu with pricing on each item, images, and more details about customization.

**Photo Gallery**: Photo sections of custom cakes, cupcakes, specialty desserts, and more.

**Contact**: Form to reach out to the business about placing an order.
